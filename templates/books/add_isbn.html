What is ISBN?

ISBN (International Standard Book Number) is a unique identifier for books. It can be either 10 or 13 digits long.

Where to Find It?
  • Back cover near the barcode
  • Copyright page inside the book
  • Online bookstore listings
  • Library catalog records
Examples:
ISBN-13: 978-6-1656-0263-1
ISBN-10: 6-1656-0263-0
